RUNNER++ is simple application that helps you to enjoy your running time. It gather statistics like speed, time, distance, average speed or calories. It is simple, intuitive, elegant and useful. All your tracks You can export into *.kml or *.rss files and upload them to the services like Google maps and so on.
Graph
Graph shows your entire route. You can switch between two types of view : Global and Tracking one. In the Global mode you can view your entire track, and on Tracking mode you can view specific point with measured speed.
To change the view you just have to sweep the finger on the screen. Pinch zoom in Global view is being used for zooming, and on the Tracking mode it is uesd to moving forward and backward.
